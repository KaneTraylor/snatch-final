How does snatch work?

You build with installments. Once you sign up, we issue an installment loan to you for 24 months at $10 a month.
We report your payment performance. Our goal with Snatch is to help you build positive payment history, safely.

Do you report to credit bureaus?

Yes - we report to one or more credit bureaus. Read more about this here.

Is this a credit card?

No, we do not offer a credit card. This is only an installment loan, which terms we issue per plan you sign up for.

Are there any other fees?

We do not charge any interest, prepayment fees, late payment fees, or anything. You only pay what you sign up for. :)

What are the courses?

The courses are curated by trained professionals on a wide array of topics. Anything from balancing a check book to how to purchase commercial real estate.

Do you offer returns on purchases?

If you're looking to return your purchase for any reason, we're here to help! We offer free refunds within 30 days. To initiate a return, please email support@snatchcredit.com.

What is a creditor?

A creditor is someone who provides credit. This includes, but is not limited to, banks, collection agencies and even car dealerships. We report your repayment progress to the credit bureaus, who then use that information to build your credit profile.

When will I see results?

Results vary for everyone. You and your credit report are unique.

Is there a credit check?

No, your credit score is just one component of your credit report. We value inclusiveness and fairness, so we leave your credit report at the door.
