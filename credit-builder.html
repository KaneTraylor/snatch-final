How Snatch helps

The Credit Account addresses two of the three key factors that determine your credit score: payment history, credit utilization, and account age.

Payment History

The most crucial aspect of your credit score is whether you consistently make timely payments on your credit cards and loans. This factor reflects your financial reliability.

Our Credit Account promotes an extensive payment history, empowering you to build your credit to its full potential.

Every month, we will report your payments to the major credit bureaus. With each report, your payment history grows, contributing positively to your credit score.

Credit Utilization

The credit utilization ratio represents the proportion of your used credit to your available credit, or credit limit. Maintaining a low ratio is beneficial for your credit health. Ideally, keeping this ratio below 10% is optimal, but it can safely go up to 30%.

Although our installment account does not currently focus on this factor, it's essential knowledge to maximize the benefits of our program and enhance your credit status. By being aware of this aspect, you can work towards optimizing your credit profile.

Account Age

The older your accounts, the better. The Credit builder account will help you build two years of history for just $10 a month.

When you initially open a Credit builder account your credit history will drop. It is due to how the credit bureaus calculate credit history. But remember this drop in credit history is only a small temporary thing. After building for a few months you will be back to normal.
